What is the Dev Derby?

  • Monthly web development contest
  • New topic every month
  • Winners announced on Mozilla Hacks
  • Advocate for the open web (not just Firefox!)

Examples

  • SnappyTree
  • The Face Builder
  • HTML5 Audio Read-Along
  • HTML5 trombone!
  • Santa's Media Queries

  • The contest ends at 11:59pm Pacific Time on the last day of the month
  • Groups and Mozilla employees are not eligible. Submit to the Demo Studio instead!
